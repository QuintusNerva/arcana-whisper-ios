Privacy Policy

Arcana Whisper — Last updated: February 16, 2026

Overview

Arcana Whisper ("the App") is a tarot and astrology companion designed with your privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. The App operates entirely on your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App uses your device's local storage (localStorage) to save:

  • Reading history — your past tarot card draws and spreads
  • Personal memory profile — themes and patterns from your readings (used to personalize future readings)
  • Reminder preferences — your daily reminder time and enabled/disabled state

All of this data is stored locally on your device only. It is never sent to our servers. You can delete all stored data at any time from the app's Settings screen.

AI-Powered Readings

The App uses OpenRouter's AI service to generate personalized tarot and astrology interpretations. When generating a reading, the App sends only card names, spread positions, and general themes — never your personal information. All connections use HTTPS encryption. Please review OpenRouter's Privacy Policy for details on their data handling.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the App uses your browser's Notification API to display local notifications. No notification data is sent to any server. You can disable reminders at any time in Settings.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics, tracking, advertising, or fingerprinting technologies. We do not use cookies, pixels, SDKs, or third-party analytics services.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Third-Party Services

The App connects to the following external services:

  • Google Fonts — to load the Cinzel and Inter typefaces (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • OpenRouter — for AI-generated tarot and astrology interpretations (card/reading context only, no personal data)

Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by Arcana Whisper, use the "Clear All Data" option in the app's Settings screen, or uninstall the App from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
