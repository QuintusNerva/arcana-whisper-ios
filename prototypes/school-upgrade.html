The Moon Card

Hidden Truths & Subconscious Fears

Tap to continue →

1 of 3

The Moon Speaks in Symbols

The Moon card (XVIII) represents the subconscious mind, illusions, and things hidden beneath the surface. When it appears in a reading, it's asking you to look deeper.

✦

Key Insight: The Moon doesn't reveal truth directly — it reveals that something is being hidden, even from yourself.

2 of 3

Upright vs. Reversed

Upright: Intuition, dreams, the unconscious. Trust your gut even when logic can't explain it.

Reversed: Release of fear, clarity returning. The fog is lifting — truth is coming to the surface.

✦

The Moon reversed is often a positive sign — it means you're finally seeing what was hidden.

3 of 3

The Moon in Spreads

In a past position: unresolved fears. In a present position: confusion or deception. In a future position: trust your intuition for what's coming.

✦

When The Moon appears with The High Priestess, your intuition is especially powerful. Pay close attention to dreams.
